 <li>
  1727 Sopranos Francesca Cuzzoni and Faustina Bordoni created a public scandal, culminating in a physical altercation
during a performance of "Astianatte".  This rivalry nearly
ruined Cuzzoni's career. She was later imprisoned for debt in the
Netherlands, and she eventually returned to Bologna where she
subsisted by making buttons.
