  1871 Birth of French baritone Eugenio GIRALDONI in Marseilles. d-Helsinki, 23 JUN 1924. He was son of baritone Leone

Barcelona [Escamillo] Carmen (1891). Sang [Scarpia]

Retired in 1921, and taught in Helsinki. Never recorded any excerpts as [Scarpia], the role he created from Tosca. MET Opera Début 28 NOV 1904 [Barnaba] La Gioconda, 1 seasons (1904-05) 26 perf., 7 works. Debut as Escamillo in Carmen (Bizet) 1891. Teacher was Caroline Ferni. Pupils were Adolf Niska, Robert Steel.
