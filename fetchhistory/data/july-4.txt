later National Opera Company (1884-89). She formed her own Emma
Juch Grand Opera Co. (1889-91), and toured the U.S. and Mexico.
She retired from the opera stage when she married (1894). Juch was
an advocate of opera in English. Made only three 
records, on 7 MAY 1904. Debut as Philine in Mignon (Thomas) JUN 1881. Teacher was Adeline Murio-Celli. Created Dora in Rinnegato (Orczy).
