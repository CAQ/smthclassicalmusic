   Richard LEWIS
  </a>

Alceste, and Idomeneus. He made his San Francisco début as [Don
José] Carmen (1955). Sang in the FP of Stravinsky's Canticum
Sacrum, Venice (1956). Teachers were Norman Allin, T. W. Evans. Pupils were David Fieldsend, Charles Walker, Keith Lewis. Created Mark in Midsummer Marriage (Tippett). Troilus in Troilus &amp; Cressida (Walton). Achilles in King Priam (Tippett).
