  2000 Death of French soprano Lillian Grandval. b. Frankfurt, 1904. She studied at the Paris conservatory. She appeared as Mimi in

[Alice], Rigoletto [Gilda], Le roi d'Ys [Rosenn], Valses de Vienne,

d'Hoffman (three soprano roles), and Fortunio [Jacqueline]. Debut as Queen in Pre aux Clercs  (Herold)
