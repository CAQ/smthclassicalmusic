  1896 Birth of French soprano Madeleine GREY in Villaines-la-Juhel, Mayenne. d-Paris, 13 MAR 1979. Was a pianist, studied at the Conservatoire but soon discovered her voice had qualities of both soprano and mezzo-soprano. Début Paris, Fauré's Mirages (1921). Friend of Ravel, and they concert toured Spain together  (1928). Ravel ask her to make the first recording of Chansons Madécasses (1932). She presented French art songs at three international festivals, Venice (1930); Siena (1932); and Florence (1934). Debut 1921. Teacher was Hettich. Pupil was Ginia Davis.
