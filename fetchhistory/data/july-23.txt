  1833 Birth of Norwegian soprano Borghild Bryhn-Langaard,  (aka. Borghild-Bryn, Borghild Langaard,

Norway. d. Oslo, Nov. 20, 1939:. Studied first in
Norway, then London, where she kept herself by working as a
governess. Début [Santuzza]; w/ John McCormack as [Turiddu]

appeared in Italy she was heard in under the name of Brunelli and at
Budapest. Her only U.S. appearance was in Chicago, Un Ballo in
Maschera, (1919). During the 1920s she was active in Scandinavia.
Her list of recordings is a short one, and many of her recordings
are Scandinavian songs.
