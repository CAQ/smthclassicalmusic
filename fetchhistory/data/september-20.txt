    1967 Death of French composer and organist
    <a href="http://www.classical-composers.org/cgi-bin/ccd.cgi?comp=mulet">
     Henri Mulet
    </a>
    in Draguignan. b-Paris, 17 OCT 1878. Choirmaster of the basilica of Sacré-Coeur, Paris. Won first prize for cello in Delsart's class in 1893 and second prize for organ in 1897. Studied organ with Guilmant and Widor. Organist in many Paris churches. Professor at the Ecole Niedermeyer and at the Schola Cantorum, 1924 to 1931. Burned his manuscripts in 1937 and left Paris for Provence. He was cathedral organist in Draguignan until 1958. Lived in seclusion and died in a convent.
